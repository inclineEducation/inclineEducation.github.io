What We Do

Having gone through the transition from high school into university, we recognize the difficulties that students have in choosing their field of study and adjusting to university life.

We host moderated forums and panel discussions in schools across Vancouver to communicate key insights to high school students about what it takes to be successful in post-secondary education and beyond.
