About Us

Helping you climb

We are a team of university students from various faculties who have come together for the sole purpose of helping students achieve more out of their university experience and beyond. Having gone through the transition from high school into university, we recognize the difficulties that students have in choosing their field of study and adjusting to university life. We hope that by holding a panel discussion where university students can talk about their programs and ambitions, students will have an easier time in this transition.


Our panel discussion consists of a team of 4 highly motivated students from different faculties in university including science, engineering, arts and business. The first half of our presentation involves our team giving advice to students about effective studying strategies, overcoming major obstacles in the university transition and extracurricular activities. Afterwards, we allow students to voice any questions or concerns that they have to our panel.


We understand that students want to be exceptional when they go into university which means that they would not want to hear about the experiences of mediocre students. That is why we have brought together a team consisting of high achieving students from various backgrounds and fields of study. We are committed to bringing the best to every event that we hold to ensure that students are able to get the most out of this experience.
