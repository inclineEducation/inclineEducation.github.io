What is Incline Education’s Mentorship Program?

Incline Education’s Mentorship Program is a platform for high-school students to be matched with a post-secondary student to guide them in their academic and career endeavours. You can sign up to be either a mentor or a mentee, and we’ll pair you up with someone who matches your interests and aspirations. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us


How Does It Work?

  1. Fill out a 2-minute survey about your interests and aspirations to help us match you with the best mentor.

  2. Within 1 week, you will receive an email from Incline Education connecting you with a mentor.

  3. Set up a time to meet your mentor and have a digital coffee chat.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I have to pay for this program?

    No! Incline Education's Mentorship Program is offered completely free of charge.



  • I haven't heard from Incline Education in a week - what should I do?

    Send us an email at contact@inclineedu.org to check-in regarding your application!



  • Who can be a mentee?

    The program is open to anyone who is considering post-secondary studies!




  • If you have any other questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us at contact@inclineedu.org.
